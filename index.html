The Story Behind ATNW

The idea for A Teacher’s Net Worth grew out of ongoing efforts to address teacher turnover, including a pivotal conversation with an exceptionally talented high school special education teacher. He candidly shared his struggle to make ends meet, feeling he had no option but to resign. He laid out his family budget, voiced his confusion about district health insurance plans, and expressed doubts about the state pension plan and other retirement tools. Ultimately, he felt trapped in a persistent state of financial distress.

Through our conversations, we walked through each of his concerns, answered his questions, corrected misperceptions, and opened new insights. We reviewed his income and debt, created a practical monthly budget, compared health, dental, and vision insurance options, explored employer-sponsored savings plans including 403(b), 457(b), and HSAs/FSAs, clarified how pensions are calculated, and discussed the value of unused leave time. Finally, we looked at how to compare public and private sector jobs and, most importantly, discussed the value proposition of teaching.

With this clearer understanding of both his financial picture and the district’s investment in him, his perspective shifted. He became more optimistic about his future ‚Äî and this exceptionally talented educator decided to stay in the profession!

That experience and many others revealed a larger opportunity to help others gain the same clarity and insight. What began as mini-sessions for all staff ‚Äî teachers, support staff and administrators ‚Äî soon grew into longer, more detailed presentations and interactive workshops. This book, along with the suite of interactive, customizable spreadsheets and other recruitment and retention tools are now coming online to reach more.

I am deeply grateful to all those who have embraced A Teacher’s Net Worth tools over the years and offered such positive feedback. It is that encouragement which has been the impetus to expand this work more broadly. I truly hope this work supports you, your district, and in turn, our profession.

The outcomes of A Teacher’s Net Worth are clear: when employees ‚Äî regardless of age, tenure, or role ‚Äî understand their salary, benefits, and overall financial situation, they feel valued beyond their contracted responsibilities. That sense of value fosters stronger engagement, deeper motivation, and lasting loyalty. For districts, the benefits are just as compelling: reduced turnover, lower absenteeism, higher productivity, and an enhanced ability to attract and retain top talent by sharing the full value of employment.

Together, these tools offer an unintimidating, engaging, and motivating way to support all employees ‚Äî while also giving districts a practical solution to strengthen retention and reduce costly turnover.
