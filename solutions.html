Products and Services

A Teacher’s Net Worth Book

This isn’t just a book — it’s a movement. This resource is a motivating guide that equips individuals on their own financial journey, along with deeply connecting them to the value of the profession. It includes a fully customizable and confidential 11-tabbed spreadsheet that provides instant, practical insights. Available in e-book and hard copy formats.

Engaging In-Person & Online Course

Say goodbye to the typical professional development and hello to transformative personal growth. Our highly praised courses — based on the ATNW book — are engaging, motivating, and impactful. Offered in English and Spanish, they can be delivered in-person and/or online to fit your district’s needs.

Total Rewards Statements

Help your employees truly understand their comprehensive value. These personalized statements are visually engaging and highlight each employee’s total compensation, fostering greater understanding across your district.

Student Loan Consultation

Webinar with a Q&A provides an overview of all federal student loan types and reviews repayment and forgiveness options. Additionally, individual sessions (15 or 30 minutes) offer a private, confidential meeting that supports borrowers identify the best solution for their individual situation.

Proactive Stay Assessments

Don’t wait for your best talent to leave to ask questions. Our stay assessments offer a proactive approach to identify and address factors impacting employee retention. This helps you keep your top performers and strategically attract new hires, strengthening your team for the long term.

Video Course

Take the ATNW journey at a pace that fits any schedule. The course includes the engaging A Teacher’s Net Worth fully customizable and confidential spreadsheet, an invaluable tool to guide users on their financial journey now and in the future.
